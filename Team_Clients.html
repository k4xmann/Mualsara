ABOUT US

OUR TEAM

Our personnel are well acquainted with the challenging needs and changing demands of today's businesses. They deal with your day to day problems and adhere to formalities in accordance to the Nigeria Customs clearance regulations and procedures of import and export of cargo. Our field representatives have also built a trust relationship with other law enforcement agencies and Port Authorities at our operational locations such as NAFDAC, Nigeria Immigration Services, NDLEA, Free Zones, Nigeria Quarantine Services, Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON), Authorities and the Nigeria Ports Authority.
