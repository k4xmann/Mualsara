Our Story

Out of the passion to offer seamless logistics services to meet the ever increasing demand for direct services to Clients and the Nigeria Ports Authority with special focus on freight forwarding, customs clearing, shipping and logistics support services, Mualsara Nigeria Limited was incorporated on 5th June 2007 under the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria with RC No. 694022.

Mualsara Nigeria Limited also known as MNL has since its inception provided advanced customer-specific value creating solutions supported by an IT based Supply Chain Management (SCM) System.



We handle services on conventional shipments and we have also contributed over years and immensely improved in managing Project cargo for clients in the Oil and Gas Industry. As a customs clearing and forwarding agent, Mualsara Nigeria Limited is fully operational in Nigeria through its offices that guarantee an effective presence of the company all across the country. 

Our experienced staff are always dedicated to provide you with logistics solutions, customized to your particular needs. Our core interest lies in serving customers with global sourcing and supply-chain management needs, creating value through innovative logistics solutions and ensuring satisfactory delivery of their total logistics requirements and services relating to their business.

OUR VISION

Our Policies

Like many business minded organizations, Mualsara Nigeria Limited has a set of policies which enhances our service performance and on which our success and survival depends on. 

Health, Safety and Environment   

Mualsara Nigeria Ltd. engages the services of a health and safety consultant on retainer-ship. The HSE unit organizes regular safety drills to Staff. The Safety Officer ensures that all personnel receive adequate safety training for the job to be done. With the team he/she reviews all the jobs before the commencement to adequately identify all potential hazards and initiate mitigation plans.

Every staff has the responsibility to work safely to protect himself/herself and others from any injury during operation and avoid having a Loss Time Injury (LTI). We are compelled to follow safety routines, processes protocols as prescribed in the Company Safety regulations. 







In the event of a hazard or damage of cargo, 

MNL has a disaster recovery plan put in place to ensure the safety of personnel and immediate remediation action for client's cargo. This is a reflection of our untiring commitment to Safety. 

During cargo operations and rigging activities, there are potential hazards. Not only do we adhere to safety standards we also appreciate environmental regulations, we more so learn from our own experiences in the shipping industry and integrate improved pollution avoidance and safety cargo handling practices into the company training programs and daily operations.

Quality Management System and Assurance

Mualsara Nigeria Ltd. ensures that our word is bond. We focus on achieving our goals to satisfy our customers and to keep to the international quality service standard by inviting external auditors to analyze operations processes and procedures.






MNL also have among the management team a seasoned Quality Management System (QMS) ISO 9001:2015 IRCA Certified Lead Auditor whose role is to guide the organization in keeping to acceptable international standards and aid her attain the complete status as an ISO 9001.2015 Certified Company. 

Competence Management and Training

As Martin Van Buren said “it is easier to do a job right than to explain why you didn't”. At MNL, it is our belief that knowledge is power. Having the right skills developed would make our staff competent in carrying out their tasks. We do not take ignorance of the law or shortcomings in technical logistics knowledge for granted.

We ensure that our staff are trained routinely, attend Seminars organized by government agencies and are updated with the latest government and international regulations.


The Team

Our personnel are well acquainted with the challenging needs and changing demands of today's businesses. They deal with your day to day problems and adhere to formalities in accordance to the Nigeria Customs clearance regulations and procedures of import and export of cargo. Our field representatives have also built a trust relationship with other law enforcement agencies and Port Authorities at our operational locations such as NAFDAC, Nigeria Immigration Services, NDLEA, Free Zones, Nigeria Quarantine Services, Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON), Authorities and the Nigeria Ports Authority.
