OUR SERVICES

Mualsara Nigeria Limited has developed an integrated end to end logistics, marine and air cargo solutions in Nigeria since its inception in 2007. we provide supply chain solutions to our customers from the source to the final destination. We are located in Port Harcourt, Lagos and Warri, Nigeria. Our logistics services comprise of customs clearances, shipping, freight forwarding, warehousing, transportation, port services, air cargo handling and exporting. We readily serve local and international markets while focusing on satisfying needs especially in the oil and gas, power and construction sectors within Nigeria. Our services include but are not limited to the following.

1. Customs Clearance
As a Licensed Customs Agent, we offer effective customs formalities and documentation ensuring prompt clearing from Sea Ports,Air Ports and Land Border entries. Containerized cargo, wet bulk, air cargo and specialized project materials areonly but a few mentioned shipments types we handle.
Experienced Customs Clearing personnel are available to assist with tariff classifications, audits and detailed costing. We have adopted business processes that interface with third party systems and accomplish excellent results thanks to our dedicated management team.
We guarantee delivery of client's cargo on the term, place and time agreed, following careful planning and execution. 

2. Freight-Forwarding and Haulage
As experts in Logistics networking, we position ourselves as a strategic partner and intermediary in your international shipments. We ensure adequate preparation of paperwork for documentation and guide on regulations and customs requirements in the country of destination. We facilitate domestic transportation by road, air, or ocean to match with your timing and cost requirements. Depending on the importance of the shipment we can organize security escorts to final destination.

3. Door to Door and Third Party Logistics
Mualsara Nigeria Limited offers Door-to-Door delivery services either as a direct request where a client desires, or when acting as a Third Party Logistics (3PL) sevice provider for our foreign partners,

4. Ship Agency, Stevedoring and Husbanding
As agents, MNL handles vessels efficiently in and out at any Nigerian Sea Port. At the dockside we husband vessels under our agency with utmost diligence, dedication and efficiency to ensure a quick and prompt berthing, stevedoring and outward dispatch to sea. We pride ourselves in providing professional Stevedoring services combined with a dedicated and knowledgeable team. Our experience and thorough understanding of operations at the Port Harcourt, Onne and Apapa Ports enables us to provide customized solutions to your logistics requirements. Our commitment to our clients the provision of reliable service.

5. Port Services
Mualsara Nigeria Limited arranges and brokers charter of ships, Tug Boats, Barges, tankers etc. for our clients. We have the technical capability to provide equipment both locally and internationally through our reputable affiliates who are major operators in the oil and gas/marine service industry. This affords us the use of these equipment locally available for immediate deployment.

6. Air Freight Handling
MNL understands that our customers demand safe, secure and swift handling of their cargo at cost effective rates. We are experienced in handling high priority and delicate shipments usually sent by air. A 72-hour advance Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) notice is enough for our seasoned air cargo staff to commence preparation to undertake clearance of these urgent shipments while respecting all government regulations and international standards in air shipment clearance and delivery.

7. Exportation
Prospective clients with export permit from the government wishing to export goods will benefit from the deep knowledge, experience and competence of our professional freight forwarding staff. Our export services team navigates the latest regulations, minimizes paperwork, expedites transit, and lowers shipping costs with truly personalized service. we also handle exportation of personal effects.

8. Value Added Services
As your choice agent Mualsara Nigeria Limited offers you free access to our years of experience to facilitate your supply chain management and logistics problems. We are accessible at all times you need our advice or visit at short notice to grasp the particular problem from your customers' point of view andproffer solutions or alternatives as soon as possible and practicable. We also provide you with assistance and guidance in respect to
-Form M and banking documentation
-Analyzing the Harmonized System Commodity Code (HSCODE) data bank
-Import/export freezones and other existing tariff concession systems.
-Customs valuation / valuation advice
-Other government agencies regulations and procedures
-Cargo consolidation and door-to-door landing costings
-Bounties and export development grants
-Export documentation and declarations (commercial invoices etc.)
-Bonded warehouse requirements and services
-In house customs duty audits
-Review of customs decisions and representation in disputes
-Arrangement of adequate warehousing services
-Priority cargo security in the warehouse and on transit
-Temporary importation (T.I.) on project materials
